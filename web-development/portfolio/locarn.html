LOCARN

Technologies Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
Devices Desktop
Project Length 2 Months

LOCARN launched a business that aimed to take on traditional courier services by offering customers the ability to schedule an hourly slot for when their parcel would be delivered.

LOCARN approached us to build a three-sided web application that would handle the company's entire technical infrastructure.

We delivered a Ruby on Rails application that consisted of a marketing site for signing up new customers; a customer portal which allowed customers to schedule and pay for their deliveries; and a management portal that tracked parcels as they progressed through the company's warehouse.

Developing such a business-critical system naturally attracts many technical challenges. We successfully overcame all of the challenges we encountered and delivered a robust web application.

Sadly LOCARN are no longer trading, but this remains one of the projects we are most proud of.
