About KOJI LABS

We are a web development studio based in Market Harborough, England. Since the company was founded in 2008, we've worked with a wide range of wonderful clients from all over the world.

Our projects come in all sorts of different shapes and sizes, from small brochure websites up to large business-critical web applications. We like a challenge and are never afraid to tackle something that's never been done before.

We're strong believers in the power of simplicty, and champion good design in all the projects we work on.
