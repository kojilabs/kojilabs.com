About KOJI LABS

We are a web design studio based in Market Harborough, England, who work with a wide range of wonderful clients from all over the world.

Our projects come in all different shapes and sizes. From small brochure websites, right up to large business-critical web applications. We like a challenge and are never afraid to tackle something new.

Our mission is to help individuals and organisations achieve their goals by creating products that delight customers.

The studio is ran by Matt West, a developer by trade and and author of a book on web design. Many projects are undertaken by Matt alone, with a team of trusted partners available to work on larger projects when needed.
