HTML5 Foundations

HTML5 Foundations is a book written by Koji Labs founder, Matt West, that teaches absolute beginners the skills they need to start building websites.

The books explores the basics of HTML5 and JavaScript, teaching the reader how to build websites by guiding them through the development of a website for a fictional pizza company.

HTML5 Foundations was published in 2012 by John Wiley & Sons. Marketing for the book was handled by John Wiley & Sons and Treehouse.

HTML5 Foundations is currently available to purchase from Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Waterstones, and on the iBook Store.
