Meet My Training Needs wanted to modernise the design of their website and make it something they're proud to send customers to.

This website was built using PHP and follows the latest HTML5 and CSS3 web standards.

For this project we worked in partnership with Zesty IT. Koji Labs was responsible for the design and development of the website with Zesty IT providing project management and support.
