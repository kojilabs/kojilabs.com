PCI Films Consulting

Technologies WordPress, PHP, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
Devices Desktop, Tablet, Mobile
Project Length 6 Months

PCI Films Consulting Ltd approached us to create a new website to showcase their range of market research reports aimed at the packaging industry.

We created a website around the popular content management system, WordPress, which allows PCI Films to easily keep content up-to-date.

We also added a new blog to keep customers engaged in what's going on at PCI Films as well as in the wider flexible packaging industry.

We decided on a clean design for the website that helps to move focus onto the content. Strong call-to-actions are used throughout the site to drive key interactions.

We followed the principles of responsive web design to create a layout that automatically adapts to the size of the device the site is being viewed on.

This website was built to the latest HTML5 and CSS3 standards.
